Game Rules

This Memory Test game has 20 levels to challenge your brain. Here's how to play:

  • Each level presents a number that you need to memorize within 5 seconds.
  • The number of digits increases with each level, starting from 1 digit and going up to 20 digits.
  • After the 5-second display, an input box will appear. Enter the number you remember.
  • Click the "Submit" button to check your answer.
  • A progress bar at the top shows your progress: green for correct answers, red for incorrect. Each of the 10 cells represents a segment of the 20 levels.

Scoring System

Your score is calculated based on the levels you successfully complete:

  • Level 1: 10 points
  • Level 2: 20 points
  • Level 3: 30 points
  • ...
  • Level 10: 100 points
  • ...
  • Level 20: 200 points
  • The total possible score is 1050 points.

Memory Performance Evaluation:

  • 840 points or higher: Excellent
  • The higher your score, the more excellent your memory!
